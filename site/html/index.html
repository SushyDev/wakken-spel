How to play:

A icehole is the middle dot of the dice, around it are the polar bears.

If the dice rolls on "5", the middle dot is the icehole and around it are 4 polar bears

If the dice rolls on "3", the middle dot is the icehole and around it are 2 polar bears

If the dice rolls on "1", the middle dot is the icehole and around it are no polar bears

If you roll any other number (“2”, “4”, “6”) that don't have a icehole in the middle it means there are also no polar bears

Extra:

You can enable penguing mode by toggling the checkmark

Penguing mode means you need to calculate all the penguings on the south pole (the opposite side of the dice) for each dice

Basic princeples:

This game is played with a minimum of 3 dice and a maximum of 12 dice, for each roll you need to calculate the polar bears (optionally penguings) for all dices on screen
